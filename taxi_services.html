SOS Auto & Taxi Service - Agra

Lapka to Aapka (Auto Service)

From Lapka to Aapka: A Journey of Change

Lapka to Aapka is a transformative project targeting auto and taxi drivers to reshape their image and improve service standards in Agra. The project focuses on training drivers to be more hospitable, customer-centric, and socially responsible, thus changing the image of "Lapka" (harasser) to "Aapka" (your friendly host). This service initiative not only boosts the local tourism industry but also enhances the overall experience of commuters and tourists.

Cultivating Trust and Hospitality

Empowering Drivers as Brand Ambassadors

Lapka to Aapka envisions creating a community of auto drivers who are recognized as brand ambassadors of the city, offering professional and courteous services to visitors. We aim to cultivate trust between tourists and local transport service providers by transforming the behavior and skills of auto drivers through targeted training.

Behavioral Change and Skills Training

Creating Role Models in the Auto Driver Community

We believe that the success of Lapka to Aapka lies in its community-focused approach. Our drivers actively participate in social initiatives such as traffic management, campaigns for cleaner streets, and anti-tobacco drives. This involvement not only enhances their personal image but also strengthens the trust between them and the passengers.

Active Participation in Social Initiatives

Enhancing Driver Reputation through Social Work

We aim to expand the Lapka to Aapka initiative to other cities, adopting a cluster-based approach for training more drivers. Our goal is to train 30 drivers in each cluster, empowering them with the skills to become true ambassadors of their cities, thus uplifting the auto-driving community and improving urban transportation across India.
