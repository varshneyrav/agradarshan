SOS Tiffin Service

An Initiative for Change

The SOS Tiffin Service, initiated by an MBA student, Manohar, is an environment-friendly initiative that provides delicious, healthy, and hygienic food to the needy. It started during the pandemic and has been a source of employment and sustenance for the community.

"Right now, we are offering 5 chapatis, 1 plate of vegetables, 1 bowl of dal, and 1 bowl of rice in a tiffin, available for the needy at just ₹35. The service operates between 10:00 am to 12:00 noon and 05:00 pm to 07:00 pm."

Innovative Eco-Friendly Approach

Recycling for a Better Tomorrow

SOS Tiffin Service is committed to environmental sustainability. Manohar's initiative collects plastic bottles filled with wrappers from households using the tiffin service. In return, customers receive a ₹5 discount on their next tiffin. The bottles are then repurposed to create eco-friendly bricks and furniture for rural communities.

Providing Employment to Families

Empowering Women in the Community

The SOS Tiffin Service not only provides food but also offers employment opportunities to family members of auto drivers who lost their jobs during the tourism downturn. Currently, five women from the auto drivers' families are involved in preparing chapatis, receiving ₹2 per chapati as compensation.

Future Expansion Plans

Bringing the SOS Initiative to More Cities

SOS Tiffin Service aims to expand its operations to cities such as Mathura, Firozabad, Jaipur, and Dholpur. The primary goal is to provide food to all in need while offering employment to those affected by the pandemic, especially those connected with the tourism industry like auto drivers, porters, and taxi drivers.
