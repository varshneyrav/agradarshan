SOS Bhojanalay Service - Agra

Affordable Meals for the Underprivileged

Nourishing Lives at Just ₹10

SOS Bhojanalay provides nutritious meals at an incredibly low cost of ₹10 to the needy at key locations across Agra. These meals are designed to be both filling and wholesome, ensuring that people facing financial hardship can access quality food daily. With this initiative, over 150 meals are served each day, helping alleviate hunger for many underprivileged individuals who depend on this service.

Empowering Communities Through Employment

Engaging Local Auto-Driver Families in Meal Preparation

SOS Bhojanalay goes beyond feeding people; it empowers local communities by offering employment opportunities. Members of the auto-driver community, who have been adversely affected by the pandemic, are actively involved in cooking and distributing food. This engagement fosters self-reliance, allows individuals to earn a livelihood, and strengthens community bonds, providing both financial support and a sense of purpose.

Sustained by Community Spirit

A Collaborative Effort for Social Change

The heart of SOS Bhojanalay lies in the active involvement of community members, from cooking to serving food. Led by a spirit of compassion, individuals and families come together to contribute to the project, ensuring its sustainability. This initiative creates a support system where both the service providers and beneficiaries are united by the common goal of fostering a healthy, hunger-free community.

Future Vision of Expansion

Spreading the Initiative Across U.P. and Rajasthan

SOS Bhojanalay aims to extend its services to other regions, including Mathura, Firozabad, Jaipur, and Dhoulpur. With a focus on providing affordable food to those in need and creating jobs for individuals who have lost work due to the pandemic, the project plans to replicate its successful model in new locations. This expansion will ensure that more communities benefit from affordable meals and opportunities for sustainable employment.
